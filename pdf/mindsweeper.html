1. This is a team event where each team consists of 5 members (same year). It consists of two rounds first MIXED BAG and second HACKBOX.
  2. The first round i.e. mixed bag will consist of questions from cryptography, basics of cyber security, Linux, Cloud and IT etc.
  3. Top teams would qualify for the second round. In second round every team would be given questions from the bandit level (question from war games ).
  4. Teams would have to open a Linux terminal window and perform some basic Linux operations. The competiti on would be an SSH provided by the site only.
  5. Students just need to follow the instructions that they get during the Bandit play and find the password from different files in SSH.
