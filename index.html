    Mind Sweeper

    1. This is a team event where each team consists of 5 members (same year). It consists of two rounds first MIXED BAG and second HACKBOX.
    2. The first round i.e. mixed bag will consist of questions from cryptography, basics of cyber security, Linux, Cloud and IT etc.
    3. Top teams would qualify for the second round. In second round every team would be given questions from the bandit level (question from war games ).
    4. Teams would have to open a Linux terminal window and perform some basic Linux operations. The competiti on would be an SSH provided by the site only.
    5. Students just need to follow the instructions that they get during the Bandit play and find the password from different files in SSH.
    6. The team that finishes the task first wins the event.

    Movie Maniac

    In this event we’ll be having a quiz on movies, The quiz will be divided into two parts-

      1. Maniacs Main-It will be a written round consisting of questions from the categories given below.
      2. Maniacs Advance- It will be a buzzer round consisting of questions from the categories given below, qualified participants from the “Main” round will be giving this.
      3.Mystery Round
    Dialogues Some special dialogues will be given from the movie to identify the movie.
    Casting Whole or partial casting will be given to identify the movie.
    Storyline Storyline will be written in terms of X and Y and they to identify the movie.
    Union Two actor will be given and participants have to list out the common movies done by them , the more you give the better you score.
    Song The name of the song will be made available to identify the movie.
    Song line The main line of the song will be given to identify the song as well as the movie.
    Sequels and prequels Movie will be given and we might ask them to write down their sequel or prequel.
    The Year A movie will be given and the participants have to write a movie name which was made the same year and was done by the same actor or actress (Akshay Kumar will definitely help them).
    The one scoring the maximum point will be the winner.

    The Big Brand Theory

    The event is all about creating your own “Big Brand” , marketing and advertisement is a part of the tasks that will follow. A fake concept of a product will be given to you and all you need to is market yourself in the real world Bidholi. Each team will be given something weird for their company as like a broken smartphone , cap of pens , notebook without pages and so on [we just need to give them the idea, and not the real world object ], the event will be of 2 hours. They have to implement 3 tasks on which their score will be marked-

      1) A facebook page
      2) Corporate table
      3) Short Ad
    Teams need to give a name to their company, should be professional but humorous.
    A facebook page- Each team needs to make their company facebook page, on which they need to list their product and the specification of it, and need to hype their page. Scoring will be done on the basis of- Number of likes on the page. The name of their company. Product listed along with its specification on the page. Users comment (the more humorous the better it is, the best comment will also be awarded, preferred language to write comment is Sarcasm).
    Corporate table – A table will be provided from our side, which they are free to place anywhere in the campus, and should customize it with whatever they get. The HQ is for telling people about their page and to get reviews on their product. Scoring will be on the basis of- How well it is customized. Number of distinct people coming to their table and enquiring about the product and reviewing it.
    Short Ad- They need to make a short ad of at least 25 seconds on the product, preferable genre will be comedy and how humorous they could create it. Scoring will done on the basis of elements of the advertisement.

    Twisters

    This will be an individual participation event comprising of two rounds. In the first round participants will be given some English as well as Hindi idioms. They have to fill in the blanks to complete the idioms. For eg. Out of the _____. The answer to this would be “blue”, thus completing the idiom “out of the blue”.
    Top 10 scorers of the first round will move on to the second round in which each participant has to frame his/her own tongue twister which should not be of not more than 10 words. Now chits numbered from 1 to 10 will be put in a bowl and each participant will be assigned a number as well. Every participant has to select a chit and if the 1st participant gets a chit numbered 5 , then contestant number on has to recite the tongue twister created by contestant number 5 . Participants will be judged upon how well they are able to recite the tongue twisters as well as on the basis of the twister they make .
